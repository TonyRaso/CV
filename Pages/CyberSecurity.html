CyberSecurityChallenge

The CyberSecurityChallenge Belgium is an annual competition designed to identify and develop cybersecurity talent in Belgium.
Participants, including students, professionals, and enthusiasts, compete in online qualifiers and a final round to showcase their skills in various cybersecurity domains.
The challenge serves as a platform for networking with industry professionals and raising awareness about cybersecurity.
